Privacy Policy

Stillix Photo Toolkit · Package: com.stillix.phototoolkit · Effective date: June 15, 2026

Stillix Photo Toolkit ("Stillix", "we", "our", or "the app") is an offline Android photo utility for batch image processing, collage creation, image information, and quick crop tools. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles photos, files, local settings, and permissions.

Short version: Stillix does not collect, sell, share, upload, or transmit personal data or photos. Image processing happens locally on your device. The app does not use ads, analytics, account login, cloud sync, or internet access.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data from users. Stillix does not operate a server and does not transmit your photos, files, identifiers, usage activity, or device information to us or to third parties.

Data type Collected by Stillix? Explanation
Photos and videos No Selected photos are processed locally on your device. They are not uploaded or sent to any server.
Personal information No No account, name, email, phone number, or address is requested by the app.
App activity or analytics No No analytics SDK, tracking SDK, advertising SDK, or crash reporting SDK is integrated.
Device identifiers No The app does not collect advertising IDs, Android IDs, or other device identifiers.

How Photos Are Handled

When you choose images, Stillix uses the Android system Photo Picker. This means you select exactly which images the app can access. Stillix reads the selected images only to perform the action you request, such as compression, resizing, format conversion, renaming, EXIF stripping, collage rendering, image information, or quick crop.

Exported files are saved locally to your device gallery using Android MediaStore scoped storage, typically under Pictures/Stillix/. Original images are not modified; Stillix saves new processed copies.

Local Data Stored on Your Device

Stillix may store app preferences and non-personal local history on your device, such as theme preference, default export quality, default export format, job type, success count, and timestamps. This information is stored locally using Android DataStore and Room database. It is not uploaded, shared, or used for tracking.

You can clear job history and app cache from the app's Settings screen. You can also remove all local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in Android system settings.

Permissions

Stillix is designed to use the minimum permissions required. Based on the current app manifest, the app does not request camera, internet, broad storage, all-files access, or all-apps query permissions.

Permission Status Reason
Camera Not requested Stillix does not take photos or use the device camera.
Internet Not requested The app works offline and does not connect to servers.
Read/write external storage Not requested Photo selection uses the system Photo Picker; exports use MediaStore scoped storage.
Manage all files Not requested The app does not need broad file management access.
Query all packages Not requested The app does not inspect installed apps.

Third-Party Services

Stillix does not include third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, social login, or cloud storage services. The app uses standard Android and AndroidX components to provide local app functionality.

Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell personal data. We do not share user data with third parties. Since Stillix does not collect user data, there is no user data for us to sell, share, or transfer.

Children's Privacy

Stillix does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any other users. The app is a general photo utility and does not include social features, user accounts, or user-generated content sharing.

Security

Stillix processes images locally on your device. Because the app does not transmit user data, there is no data transfer from the app to our servers. Local files and app data remain under the protection of your Android device and operating system.
